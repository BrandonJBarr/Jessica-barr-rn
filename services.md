## [SECTION 1: EXPERT SERVICES]

### 1. Standard-of-Care Analysis & Merit Review
I don’t just read the records—I map them against established pediatric and neonatal clinical guidelines. My review identifies specific deviations from the standard of care, ensuring you have a clear, evidence-based roadmap for your case merit assessment. 
*   **Outcome:** A high-level, candid evaluation that saves your firm time by identifying strengths and potential vulnerabilities early.

### 2. Jury-Ready Chronology Development
Dense, disjointed medical charts are the enemy of compelling testimony. I transform voluminous records into clean, chronological timelines that highlight critical clinical events, missed observations, and deviations in nursing judgment.
*   **Outcome:** A structured, defensible narrative that simplifies complex clinical data into a clear story for judge and jury.

### 3. Clinical Research & Literature Support
In complex litigation, the standard of care is often found in the nuance of clinical literature. I synthesize current clinical research, hospital policy, and nursing protocols to substantiate the testimony, ensuring your legal theory is anchored in rigorous evidence.
*   **Outcome:** Strong, evidence-based foundations that hold up under the pressure of cross-examination.

### 4. Deposition & Trial Testimony
As an Assistant Clinical Professor, I excel at teaching complex topics to those without a medical background. My testimony is objective, calm, and firmly grounded in both academic theory and years of high-acuity pediatric clinical experience.
*   **Outcome:** Authoritative testimony that remains accessible, credible, and focused on the clinical facts of the case.
