Standard-of-Care Analysis & Merit Review

I map clinical records against established pediatric and neonatal guidelines to identify specific deviations. This ensures you have a clear, evidence-based roadmap for your case merit assessment from the outset.

Outcome A high-level, candid evaluation that identifies strengths and potential vulnerabilities early, saving your firm valuable time.

Jury-Ready Chronology Development

I transform dense, disjointed medical charts into clean, chronological timelines that highlight critical clinical events, missed observations, and deviations in nursing judgment.

Outcome A structured, defensible narrative that simplifies complex clinical data into a clear story for the judge and jury.

Clinical Research & Literature Support

I synthesize current clinical research, hospital policy, and nursing protocols to substantiate testimony, ensuring your legal theory is anchored in rigorous evidence.

Outcome Strong, evidence-based foundations that hold up under the pressure of intense cross-examination.

Deposition & Trial Testimony

As an Assistant Clinical Professor, I excel at teaching complex topics to those without a medical background. My testimony is objective, calm, and firmly grounded in both academic theory and clinical practice.

Outcome Authoritative testimony that remains accessible, credible, and focused on the clinical facts of the case.
