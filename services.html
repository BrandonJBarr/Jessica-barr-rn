Specialized Expert Services

When the standard of nursing care is scrutinized, the difference between a successful case and a failed one often comes down to clinical clarity. I provide high-authority record review and testimony grounded in both active clinical education and over 16 years of pediatric excellence.

Merit Screening & Case Assessment

Rapid, high-level clinical assessment to determine if a case meets the threshold for nursing standard of care violations. I help counsel decide early whether to commit resources to complex litigation.

Clinical Chronologies & Fact-Finding

Transforming thousands of pages of medical records into clear, logical, and evidence-based timelines. I identify the "invisible" breaches in care that are often buried in dense nursing notes and flowsheets.

Standard of Care Analysis

Authoritative analysis of nursing protocols, hospital policies, and administrative standards. As a Clinical Professor, I am uniquely positioned to evaluate whether a nurse's actions aligned with the required educational and professional benchmarks.

Deposition & Trial Testimony

A teacher’s voice in the courtroom. I provide objective, well-prepared, and highly credible testimony that distills complex medical concepts into simple, understandable truths for juries and mediators.

Specialized in Pediatrics

From Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU) to Acute Cardiac disorders and Pediatric Home Health, I provide expert insight across the entire spectrum of pediatric nursing.
