Nurse Expert Witness Services

When the standard of nursing care is in question, you need clarity, accuracy, and an authoritative voice. I provide specialized clinical insight and litigation support for legal teams, helping you navigate complex medical documentation and expert testimony.

My Core Services

Case Merit Review

Don't waste time on cases lacking clinical foundation. I provide a high-level initial assessment of medical records to help you determine case merit quickly and efficiently.

Clinical Chronology & Timeline Creation

Complex medical records are often difficult to digest. I synthesize vast amounts of data into clean, logical chronologies that clearly highlight breaches in the standard of care.

Medical Record Analysis

I conduct meticulous reviews of hospital charts, diagnostic imagery, and depositions to identify critical clinical data that could be the turning point in your case.

Deposition & Trial Support

From helping counsel prepare for key clinical depositions to providing clear, authoritative, and professional courtroom testimony.

Expert Report Drafting

Clear communication is essential. I draft professional, evidence-based reports that outline clinical findings, standard-of-care analysis, and deviations in care.
